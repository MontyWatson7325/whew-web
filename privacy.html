Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 28, 2026

Whew ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use the Whew app ("App").

Information We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to provide the Whew experience:

  • Ancestor information you provide: Names, approximate dates, and locations you enter to personalize your experience. This information is stored on your device and, if you choose to create an account, on our servers.
  • Usage data: Anonymized information about how you interact with the app (scenarios played, session length) to improve the experience. This data cannot be used to identify you personally.
  • Account information (optional): If you create an account, we collect your email address to manage your account.

FamilySearch Integration

Whew integrates with FamilySearch to help connect your family history to historical scenarios. If you choose to link your FamilySearch account:

  • We access only the family history data you explicitly authorize.
  • We do not store your FamilySearch credentials.
  • You can revoke access at any time through your FamilySearch account settings.
  • FamilySearch's own Privacy Policy governs any data they hold.

How We Use Your Information

  • To generate historically grounded scenarios personalized to your family history
  • To save your progress and session history within the app
  • To improve app performance and the quality of historical content
  • To communicate with you about your account (if applicable)

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes. We may share anonymized, aggregated data (which cannot identify you) for analytics purposes.

We use FamilySearch's API as an authorized integration partner. No other third-party data sharing occurs without your explicit consent.

Data Storage and Security

Ancestor information you enter is stored locally on your device by default. If cloud sync is enabled, data is transmitted securely using industry-standard encryption (TLS). We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information.

Children's Privacy

Whew is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time by contacting us. If you delete the app, locally stored data is removed from your device.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes through the app or via email. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
